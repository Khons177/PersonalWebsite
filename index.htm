Young Rewired State 2014

For Young Rewired State Fesival of Code 2014, I collaborated with 6 others to create an internet portal for the elderly. It contains a news feed of vunerabilities; an application manager, and a personalised dashboard with a temperature recording of your home (to demonstrate the future applications of the dashboard), a link with dropbox and a wordsearch.
